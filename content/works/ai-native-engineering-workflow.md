An ongoing investment in the loop itself: how fast an idea becomes a verified
change, and how little of that speed is paid for with future debugging. The
goal is leverage without losing the thread of what the code is doing.

## Role

Accelerate engineering execution with agentic tools and automation — without
trading away traceability.

## System

- **Terminal-first workflows** that keep the feedback loop tight.
- **AI-assisted editing** for the mechanical parts of a change.
- **Repeatable scripts** so routine work stops being a decision.
- **Structured knowledge surfaces** that make context retrievable later.

## Impact

Reduced **iteration cost** while keeping implementation **traceable and
maintainable** — speed that compounds instead of accruing as debt.
