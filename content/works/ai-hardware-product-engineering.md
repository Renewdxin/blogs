A companion-product line that had to feel responsive on-device while still
reaching into cloud models when the task demanded it. The work sat at the seam
between firmware behaviour, signal handling, and the interaction model a person
actually experiences.

## Role

Bridge software, hardware signals, and AI interaction design — translating
fuzzy product intent into systems that behave predictably on real hardware.

## System

- **Local-first device flows** so the product stays usable without a round trip.
- **Cloud model routing** that escalates to larger models only when needed.
- **Memory surfaces** that keep context coherent across sessions.
- **Fallback states** for degraded connectivity, latency spikes, and cold starts.

## Impact

Moved exploratory companion-product concepts from demo-grade sketches toward
**testable engineering prototypes** — artifacts the team could measure, break,
and iterate on instead of argue about.
